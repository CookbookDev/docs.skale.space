MetaKeep provides full self-custody of assets without the need for a hardware wallet. They achieve this with a secure, decentralized virtual environment. Their reliable and cost-effective innovations promote mass adoption, and they offer FDIC-inspired insurance policies to enhance user confidence.

## Implementation Example

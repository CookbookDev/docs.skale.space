Keepsake is a marketplace built on two distinct SKALE Chains: Calypso and Nebula.

It enables users to buy and sell NFTs and incorporates the SKALE metaport widget, allowing free bridging of various ERC-20 tokens between the SKALE Chains.

For more information please check one of the two marketplaces:
- [Keepsake Calypso Marketplace](https://skale.keepsake.gg/)
- [Keepsake Nebula Marketplace](https://nskale.keepsake.gg/)
